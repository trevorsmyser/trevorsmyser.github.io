Buffalo Chicken Dip

Whether it's a party, or a night at home, Buffalo Chicken Dip
is an easy dish that anyone will enjoy. Get the flavor of
Buffalo Wings straight from your slow cooker with this simple recipe.
Find the Ingredients and Directions below:

Ingredients:

  • 2 (10 ounce) cans of chunk chicken, drained
  • 3/4 cup of Frank's Red Hot
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages of cream cheese
  • 1 cup of ranch dressing
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 bunch of celery, cut to 4 in. pieces
  • 1 (8 ounce) box of chicken-flavored crackers

Directions:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat the chicken and Frank's Red Hot sauce in a skillet for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add in ranch and cream cheese and stir.
  4. Continue to cook and stir for 3-5 minutes with medium heat.
  5. Add half of your cheddar cheese, and transfer the mixture to your slow cooker.
  6. Before covering, add the second half of the cheddar cheese.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 35-40 minutes.
  8. Serve with celery sticks and crackers.

This has been one of my favorite dishes to make lately. So whether it may be for a party, or just an easy meal at home, this Buffalo Chicken Dip is the recipe for a great night.
I always make sure to use Frank's Red Hot rather than any other sauce. To me, it makes this dish taste so much better compared to any other sauce. Another ingredient I always use to make this dish is Great Value Mild Cheddar Cheese. I have experimented with other choices, and I have honestly not seen much of a difference between them. So, I typically go with this cheese, because it just as good as the others, but it is cheaper.
